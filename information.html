About Us :


HINDU COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY is a premier institution dedicated to providing quality education.>Established in 1999, Hindu College of Engineering and Technology is located in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh.

We are being a part of Indian Education system from 1860 (162 Years ago) as a Sanskrit School. Later it was transformed to Town High School i.e., Hindu High School Guntur.



Our Mission :


To foster academic excellence and innovation. Empowering Minds, Enriching Lives .

Hindu College of Engineering and Technology's (HCET) mission is to provide "Skill-Based Technical Engineering Education" to all students from diverse backgrounds across the country. They aim to achieve this by equipping students with experienced faculty and modern lab facilities ¹.

The college is part of the Hindu College Committee, which has a rich history dating back to 1860 when it was established as a Sanskrit School. Over the years, the institution has evolved, and today, HCET is a renowned engineering college offering various undergraduate and postgraduate programs.
